Eighteenth International Conference on Interdisciplinary Social Sciences

The conference program groups together presentations along similar themes to facilitate knowledge sharing and community building. The first schedule of sessions will be announced two months prior to the conference start date. In our blended conference model, we will offer both place-based and online options for viewing and engaging

2023.7.19(WED)- 21(FRI)

@ Oxford Brookes University, Oxford, UK + Online

Blended Ways of Speaking

Plenary Sessions

Plenary speakers, chosen from among the world’s leading thinkers, offer formal presentations on topics linked to annual themes.

  • In-Person: A traditional lecture format
  • Online: The plenary sessions will be delivered as a mix of live or recorded videos for the delegates.

Garden Conversation

Garden Conversations are open, informal sessions that allow Research Network Members a chance to meet Plenary Speakers.

  • IIn-Person: An informal discussion space in a non-traditional setting.
  • Online: The Garden Sessions will be delivered as a mix of live or recorded videos for the delegates.

Talking Circles

Talking Circles offer an opportunity to meet other delegates with similar interests and concerns.

  • In-Person: An in-person space to meet, framed by thematic areas
  • Online: An online space to meet framed by thematic areas.

Themed Paper Presentations

This type of session is best suited for reports on completed research or scholarly work.

  • The formal oral presentation of work should be limited to 20 minutes.
  • Online: We recommend a full presentation of no more than 20 minutes.

Innovation Showcase

Researchers and innovators present practices, products or research, and development.

  • 20 minute presentation of case studies, process and product showcase, pop-up exhibition, show-and tells, etc.
  • Online: Video demonstrations, online galleries, etc.
